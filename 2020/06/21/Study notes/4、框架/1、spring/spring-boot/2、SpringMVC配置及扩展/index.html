结合源码和文档分析：SpringBoot对SpringMVC做了哪些配置，哪些封装，以及如何扩展?

首先要掌握springmvc的原理。

文档：https://docs.spring.io/spring-boot/docs/2.0.0.RELEASE/reference/htmlsingle/#boot-features-spring-mvc

springboot对springmvc的配置的精髓：原文翻译

1、Spring Boot provides auto-configuration for Spring MVC that works well with most applications.
(springboot自动配置好了springmvc)

2、The auto-configuration adds the following features on top of Spring’s defaults:(以下是springboot对springmvc的默认配置：)

3、Inclusion of ContentNegotiatingViewResolver and BeanNameViewResolver beans.

//自动配置视图解析器ViewResolver
//ContentNegotiatingViewResolver 所有视图解析器的组合。
//如何定制?：自己配置一个视图解析器，ContentNegotiatingViewResolver 就会将其注册到容器列表中

4、Support for serving static resources, including support for WebJars (covered later in this document)).
//静态资源的支持 WebJars 的支持

5、Automatic registration of Converter, GenericConverter, and Formatter beans.
//自动注册了参数转换器，Formatter 格式化器 在spring.mvc.date-format可以指定
//如何定制?创建一个参数或者日期类型转化器注册到spring容器中

6、Support for HttpMessageConverters (covered later in this document).
//SpringMVC用来转换http请求和响应。
eg:返回user对象，要用json返回到前端。

定制：自己添加一个HttpMessageConverters ，注册到spring容器中

7、Automatic registration of MessageCodesResolver (covered later in this document).
//定义错误代码生成规则

8、Static index.html support.
//静态资源支持

9、Custom Favicon support (covered later in this document).
//自定义标签图片支持

10、Automatic use of a ConfigurableWebBindingInitializer bean (covered later in this document).
//web数据绑订器

Springboot如何扩展MVC配置:编写一个配置类 MyApplicationConfig 是WebMvcConfigurerAdapter 类型
